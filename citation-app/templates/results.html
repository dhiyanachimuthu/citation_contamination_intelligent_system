Risk Model & Methodology

Risk Formula:

risk_score = (1/hop) × sentiment_weight × log(1 + citation_count)
if retracted → × 2.0
sentiment:  {Endorsing: 1.5,  Neutral: 1.0,  Critiquing: 0.0}
hop_weight: {hop1: 1.0,  hop2: 0.5,  hop3: 0.33}

Contamination Score KPI: (HIGH×1.0 + MEDIUM×0.6 + LOW×0.3) / total × 100

Sentiment Classification: Endorsing = confirms/replicates findings · Neutral = background citation · Critiquing = negative trial / explicit refutation (→ SAFE)

Data Sources: OpenCitations COCI · Semantic Scholar · Retraction Watch (60,936 indexed DOIs)
