How it works
  1. DOI is validated and normalized
  2. Retraction Watch index is checked (O(1) lookup)
  3. OpenCitations fetches real citing papers — up to 3 hops, max 150 nodes
  4. Semantic Scholar enriches each node with title, authors, citation count
  5. Risk scores are computed & papers ranked by contamination influence
  6. Interactive graph & ranked table are rendered
